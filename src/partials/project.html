Country villa

Country villa of 500m2 for a family of 5 people. The peculiarity of the layout is that in the center of the house there is an "open living room", the center of the entire composition, opening panoramic windows in good weather, the living room is transformed into a courtyard. Living rooms are placed in separate volumes that create the outline of the house. On the second floor there is a large library with a fireplace.
